# InsightClass AI - Sample Questions

## Fast Response Questions (Optimized)

### Individual Student Queries
- "Give me academic score of R-1000"
- "What is the attendance of R-1003?"
- "Show me participation rate for R-2500"
- "Tell me the risk level of R-1500"

### High-Risk/Urgent Queries (Pre-filtered for speed)
- "Which students need immediate attention?"
- "Show me high risk students"
- "Who is at risk in my class?"
- "List students needing attention"

### Quick Class Overview
- "How many students are high risk?"
- "What's the overall class attendance?"
- "How many students are stable?"
- "Give me a quick class summary"

### Pattern-Based Queries
- "How many quiet contributors do I have?"
- "Show me consistent high-engagers"
- "Which students show fragile engagement?"
- "List inconsistent effort students"

## Slower Response Questions (More Processing)

### Detailed Analysis
- "Give me a complete profile of R-1000"
- "Analyze the learning patterns in my class"
- "What should I focus on this week?"
- "Create a 7-day teaching plan"

### Comparative Queries
- "Compare R-1000 and R-1003"
- "Which pattern has the most students?"
- "Show me trends across all students"

## Tips for Fastest Responses

1. **Ask about specific students** - Uses targeted data injection
2. **Use keywords like "high risk" or "attention"** - Pre-filtered results
3. **Request single metrics** - "academic score" not "complete profile"
4. **Limit scope** - "Top 5" instead of "all students"

## Response Time Estimates

- Single student metric: ~1-2 seconds
- High-risk list (top 10): ~2-3 seconds
- Class summary: ~3-4 seconds
- Detailed analysis: ~5-7 seconds
- Complex comparisons: ~7-10 seconds
